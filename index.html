About Bolang Travel

Founded in 2025, Bolang Travel (OPC) Private Limited focuses on building digital products that connect travelers with local communities and experiences. Our flagship product, OmyTribe, enables users to join groups, share images and locations in chat, create and manage events, and discover nearby tribes.

OmyTribe — Travel Tribe App

OmyTribe is a community-driven app for backpackers and hostel travelers. It supports OTP login, group chat, event creation, and location sharing, with a focus on safety and local discovery.

Learn more at omytribe.app.

  • Join and explore nearby tribes
  • Create & discover events
  • Share images and locations in chat
  • QR-based hostel referrals
  • iOS & Android support
